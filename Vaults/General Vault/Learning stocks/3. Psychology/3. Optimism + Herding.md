Herding Bias/Optimism
- Herding: Following what everyone else is doing rather than coming up with your own process/analysis
- 90% of traders fail (buy high/sell low)
	- if everyone is set up to fail, why follow what they do?
- Natural for the human mind to compare and agree with others rather than formulate your own
- FOMO and natural to be influenced by others
	- Ex. GME rally
- Following the herd is a significant driver to market bubbles (rallies and crashes)
- Heading leads to extreme optimism
- Extreme optimism leads to bubbles
	- market reversals
- Everyone is either really long or everying is really short
- Don't follow the crowd
