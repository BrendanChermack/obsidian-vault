- Trades process so much info on a daily basis
	- numbers, quotes, plans, charts, etc.
- When you process too much info or too many tasks at once, you will not be able to retain that info
	![[Pasted image 20250416093100.png]]
- More time working on a task doesn't equate to more productivity and efficiency
- There is a threshold where the time spent on completing a task meets your max capabilities of working efficiently
	- a break is needed at that threshold to refresh
- Figure out your threshold
