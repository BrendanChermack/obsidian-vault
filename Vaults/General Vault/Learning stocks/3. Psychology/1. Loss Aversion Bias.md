## Avoiding Loss Aversion Bias
1. Trading is an emotional battle: you vs yourself
	- if you let your emotions take over your decision making, your trading performance will suffer
	- It's not you vs the market
2. Losses are a part of trading
	- winners cannot exist without losers
	- you have to lose in order to win
3. When you lose, view it as a win if you followed your plane and if you learned something from it
	- Journaling will help
	- action + reflection = progress
	- if you don't learn from your losses and mistakes no progress will occur
- Once you understand and accept that you will free your emotions of fearing to lose (loss aversion)
- Without execution + risk, trading would not exist
- Losing is a part of the plan
- All you can do is follow the plan and stay patient and disciplined (no emotions involved)
