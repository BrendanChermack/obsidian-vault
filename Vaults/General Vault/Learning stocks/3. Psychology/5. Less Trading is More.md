## Less trading is more trading
- You don't need to trade everyday, trade when your edge is present
	- if no edge = no trade
- You get paid for your opportunity not your time
- Quality over quantity
- DONT DO THIS
	- 5 trades in a day
		- 4 low quality trades
		- 1 high quality trade
 - DO THIS
	- 5 trades in WEEK
		- 0 low quality trades
		- 5 high quality trades
- Let the market come to you
- There is no rush
	- Open a lot of time and opportunities
- Trades your setups and only your setups
