## Why to never give up
- Many people think its easy
- They think they will be the best in weeks or a months time
- You are not going to consistently make money right off the bat
- Screen time, experience and time in the market will allow you to learn
- Learn by taking losses
- No one knows what the market is going to do next
- Being a trader takes 2+ to fully mature and understand the game
- Don't give up
