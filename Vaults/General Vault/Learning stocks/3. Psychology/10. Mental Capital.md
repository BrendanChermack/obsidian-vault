- Traders have typical capital along with mental capital
- Mental capital
	- your mental capabilities to work efficiently and a reflection of you trading mindset and self-image
		- doubt, fears, IMPATIENCE, DISCIPLINE, FOCUS, etc.
- Mental capital is important to preserve when the market is choppy and no setups are presenting themselves
	- When there is no opportunity
- Mental capital is just as important as typical capital

Way to protect your mental capital
- Walk away
- **Journal + reflect**
- Find when your mental capital is depleting
- **Avoid beating your self up**
	- constructive thought process on way to strengthen your mindset
- **Read trading psychology books**
	- Brett Steenbarger (daily trading coach, psychology 2.0), Mark Douglas (trading in the zone)
