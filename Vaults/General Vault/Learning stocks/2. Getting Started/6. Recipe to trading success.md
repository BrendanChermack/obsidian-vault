Trading is all about you
- Marketing understanding * you = profit and results
- Market understanding (100%) * you(50%) = 50% results
- Market understanding (50%) * you(100%) = 50% results
- Equally important to learn the you part
- Crossing the finish line to profitability comes from you
Consistency
- Observe the market (market understanding)
	- proper trading plan (market understanding)
- Take low risk and high reward trading setups (market understanding)
- **Keep emotions in check** (you)
- **Leave opinions out of the picture** (you)

Emotional intelligence
- Listen to the market and observe/follow what the market is telling you

Not letting emotions control the situation
- Easy for your observations of the market to be skewed based on your emotion
	- based on what you think is going to happen
- Focus on factual info about the markets not off opinions/emotions
- **Your observation of the market will be skewed if you let you: anger, fear, greed, hesitation over-confidence control your decision making/view point of the market**

Self discovery in trading
- Emotions will complicate your decision making process
- Study your self
- **Log emotions after a trade**

Losing trades aren't losing trades if you learn from them
- Lose to succeed
- A losing trade will not define your success
- Grow from the loss
