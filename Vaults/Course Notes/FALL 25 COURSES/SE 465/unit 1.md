- product vision
	- starting point for a software product development is a product vision
	- nothing directly for software
		- marketing and what the product is
	- the product vision should answer three questions
		- what is the product?
		- who is the targeted customers?
		- why should customers buy the product?
- Moore's vision template
	- FOR - targeted customer
	- WHO - need or opportunity
	- THE - product name is a product category
	- THAT - key benefit, compelling reason to buy
	- UNLIKE -primary competitive alternative
	- OUR PRODUCT - statement of primary differentiation

- software product management
	- business activity that focuses on the software products to be developed and sold by the business
	- product manager
		- takes overall responsibility for the product and are involved in planning, dev, and product marketing
	- must look outwards to customers and potential customers rather than focusing on the software
	- oversee it but not direct development of it
